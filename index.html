Biography

Mingjie Wang is currently a 4th-year Ph.D. student at College of Materials Science and Technology of Beijing Forestry University, under the supervision of Prof. Jianmin Gao. He was admitted by Beijing Forestry University in 2016 as a M.Eng. student and converted into a Ph.D. student in 2018. Before that, he earned his B.Eng. degree from Northeast Forestry University in 2016, where he worked with Prof. Yingcheng Hu.
